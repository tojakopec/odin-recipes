Ingredients

  • 1kg cream cheese
  • 7 eggs
  • 300-400g of sugar (depending on desired sweetness)
  • 1 Tbsp of flour
  • 200ml heavy cream

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 210Â°C.
  2. Combine all the ingredients in a bowl until smooth and creamy
  3. Line a springform with parchment paper extending out past the edges of the pan
  4. Bake on the center rack for 35-50 minutes (until burnt on top)
  5. Cool at room temperature, then transfer to the fridge
